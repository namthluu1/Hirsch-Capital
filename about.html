About Us

Who We Are

Hirsch Capital is a student-led investment fund at ESSEC Business School, founded to empower students with practical financial experience. We are driven by a passion for learning, teamwork, and long-term value investing. Our members come from diverse academic and cultural backgrounds but share a common commitment to excellence and intellectual curiosity.

What We Do

We simulate real-world investment management by running a student-operated portfolio, researching public equities, and presenting investment theses. Members undergo training in financial modeling, industry analysis, and macroeconomic research.
