First Student-Led Investment Fund on ESSEC's Campus

Hirsch Capital is a student-led investment initiative focused on financial education, collaborative research, and portfolio management. Through simulated investment strategies and real-world analysis, our members develop strong technical skills and a deep understanding of markets. At the core of Hirsch Capital is a commitment to rigorous learning, teamwork, and preparing the next generation of finance professionals.
